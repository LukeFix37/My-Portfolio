These are some photos showcasing some of the things that I have done with HPU Minds and our Arachnaphobia Exposure Therapy VR project:

Our Team:

This is a photo of our team at HPU Minds. We are a team of High Point University computer science and interdisciplinary students who are working with Steve Wozniak (High Point University's Innovator In Residence) to design and create exciting new projects. So far projects have included aspects such as computer vision, artificial Intelligence, electrical and mechanical engineering. Working alongside Computer Science faculty and with mentoring from the co-founder of Apple, our team gains not only technical experience but also valuable life skills such as effective communication, teamwork, and time management. The Woz Project gives undergraduate students the opportunity to use the concepts they learn in the classroom and apply them in an innovative, creative and collaborative environment.

Lectures:

This is a photo of our team listening to a lecture from Steve Wozniak. We are learning about his experiences as an engineer and how important the work we are doing as a team really is. He also offered a lot of career advice and shared a lot of good input


Soldering:

Some parts of the project including soldering wires and connecting them to a skin sensor. This way we were able to work with arduino to code the sensor to detect how the user's skin reacts to the VR simulation.
